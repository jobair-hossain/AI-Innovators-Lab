About the Camp

AI Innovators Lab is a short summer experience that introduces students to the foundations of coding and artificial intelligence in an engaging and an early age coding environment. The camp combines guided instruction, interactive exercises and project-based learning, so students can explore how computers solve problems and how simple AI systems are built.

Camp Purpose: To provide instruction in foundational programming and introductory artificial intelligence concepts for young learners through hands-on activities and guided project work, while encouraging curiosity, creativity, and early interest in computing.

Daily Schedule

The camp follows a four-day progression from coding foundations to guided AI exploration and final student presentation.

Day 1 — July 6

Theme: Introduction to Programming

  • Camp welcome and check-in
  • Overview of computers, coding, and problem solving
  • Beginner programming concepts through guided activities
  • Interactive practice and group discussion

Day 2 — July 7

Theme: Data and Patterns

  • Introduction to data, examples, and simple patterns
  • How computers use information to make decisions
  • Hands-on learning with beginner-friendly exercises
  • Team-based activities and guided exploration

Day 3 — July 8

Theme: Beginner AI Project Work

  • Introduction to a simple AI-based activity or model
  • Team project planning and guided development
  • Practice with coding, testing, and refinement
  • Preparation for final presentation

Day 4 — July 9

Theme: Project Showcase and Celebration

  • Final project completion and review
  • Student team presentations
  • AI Showcase activity
  • Camp wrap-up and recognition
